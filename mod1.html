Welcome to commit to be fit! There are all kinds of extreme exercise programs that attempt to change lifestyles but have little success in a lifestyle change. This program is designed to demystify how to loose weight. Specifically this course is designed for adults who need to lose 10-40lbs. No matter what your reason for wanting to lose this weight: heart health, joint pain, or you just want to look better naked our goal is 2 pounds a week. This is the kind of weight loss that will lead you to live life differently, and keep the weight off. The plan is simple: Eat Less, Move More and Get Help!
