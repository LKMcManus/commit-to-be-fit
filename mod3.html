You must measure your cardio fitness regularly to see whether or not you’re getting better! Gym employees can help you evaluate your body composition and fitness levels, but you don’t have to be a member of a gym to test your abilities!

Start with the simple cardio tests below so you can benchmark where you plan to progress.

- The 3-Minute Step Test - Click here to learn how to do this test and see where you stack up.

- The 1-Mile Walk Test - - Click here to learn how to do this test and see where you stack up.

These tests are great tools to see how you are doing. As long as you are getting better and losing weight, your fitness plan is working! If you find you aren’t getting better, you might consider making some changes to your diet or switching up your gym routine!

By the end of this section, you will have the tools to be able to implement several cardio exercises in your gym routine and lose weight with improved eating habits.
