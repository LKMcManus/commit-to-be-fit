Plateaus?

  • What is a plateau?
  • What can you do about it?
  • Objective: Implement techniques to maintain goal weight.

Audio

Captions

What is a plateau? A plateau occurs when you aren’t progressing despite the fact that you think you’re doing “all of the right things.” Our bodies go from losing weight consistently to getting stuck at a certain number. So what can you do about it?

  1. Track your meals.
  2. Think about how your work outs have been.
  3. Do not skip out on sleep!

Within this section, we will prepare you to face plateaus to reach your goals and then maintain your goal body weight.
