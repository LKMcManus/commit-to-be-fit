Cardio: Eating small, eating often

  1. Know how many calories to eat to lose weight (Women eat a minimum of 1,200 calories per day; Men eat a minimum of 1,500 calories per day).
  2. Divide your total calories for the day by 5.
  3. Look at meal plan examples online.

Captions

According to Livestrong, in order to lose weight, you need to consume 500 – 1000 calories less than your normal intake but don’t go overboard. Women need a minimum of 1,200 calories a day, and men need a minimum of 1,500 calories a day. Once you know how many overall calories to eat, divide up your total calories into five small meals a day. Now, you know how many calories to eat at each meal so start reading food labels, paying attention to serving sizes and begin meal planning! There are many meal plan examples online to help as you get started. Make sure that you are getting plenty of fruits and vegetables with smaller amounts of protein and grains!
