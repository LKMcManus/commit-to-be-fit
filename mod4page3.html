Now that you have done all this work to lose your weight in a healthy way, you need to keep it off! We all have a tendency to backtrack and do what is called the yo-yo effect. In order to avoid the yo-yo, follow these techniques:

  1. Commit whole-heartedly to your fitness journey!
  2. Learn from your own behavior– journal to see when you started adding pounds back on.
  3. Weigh yourself and monitor any gains so you can take action if you are above your goal weight.
  4. It’s not a diet. It’s a lifestyle! Make sensible eating a part of your life longterm.
