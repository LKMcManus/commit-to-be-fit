The American College of Sports Medicine has the following recommendations for adults in cardiovascular exercise, strength training and stretching.

Cardiovascular Exercise 30-60 minutes of moderate intensity 5 days a week or 20-60 minutes of vigorous-intensity 3 days a week. You can break up the recommendation into 10 minute segments throughout the day You want to gradually progress your exercise time, frequency and intensity

Strength Training Train each muscle group 2 to 3 days each week utilizing a variety of exercises. 2- 4 sets of each exercise is enough 8-12 reps will improve strength and power. 10-15 reps will be a good stating place for most. 15-20 reps will move muscle improvement from strength and power to muscular endurance. Wait 48 hours between working major muscle group.

Stretching exercise 3 to 5 times per week. Hold stretch for 10-30 seconds. Repeat each stretch 2 to 4 times. Get at least 60 seconds of stretching for each exercise.
