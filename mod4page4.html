Now that you have met your goal weight, it is time to set new goals! Using the SMART technique for setting goals is a great way to continue making progress.
  • S – Specific –Your goal should be clear and easy to understand.
  • M – Measurable –Making your goal measurable means adding a number of pounds to lose.
  • A – Attainable –Before you can add a number, you have to know how high or low you want to go.
  • R – Relevant –Set goals that are important to where you are in your life right now.
  • T – Time-Bound –Include an end-point. Knowing that you have a deadline motivates you to get started.
