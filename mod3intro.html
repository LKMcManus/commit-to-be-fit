If you are taking this program than you have a common goal with most adults. You have 10-40 pounds you want to lose. In order to do so we have to eat less, move more and get help. We all like freedom to choose how to reach our goals. Either choice is a fine one so long as you honor the overall principle that you have to burn more calories than you consume. You have a choice within our program to lose 2 pounds a week until you reach your goal. Read the descriptions to the left and decide if low fat foods and cardio exercise is more your preference or low carb foods and strength training exercise. Then as you go check out reference links to get help.
