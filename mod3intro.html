If you are taking this program, then you have a common goal with most adults. You have 10-40 pounds you want to lose. In order to do so, you have to eat less, move more and get help. We all like freedom to choose how to reach our goals. You have a choice within our program on how to lose the 2 pounds a week until you reach your goal. If you would like to focus on cardio and maintain a more regular intake of food, click the cardio button. If you would like to focus on building strength and don't mind considering a low-carb diet, click the strength button. Either choice is a fine one; as long as you honor the overall principle that you have to burn more calories than you consume. As you go through either module, check out the reference links to get help.
