Cardio: Beyond running

exercise video (jumping jacks, squats, side lunges, burpees).

Audio

Captions

If you aren’t into running to get your cardio workout, that’s okay! There are plenty of exercises you can do to get the same, if not better, cardio results. Our example is doing jumping jacks. Do those for 2 minutes. Follow it up with squats making sure to keep your knees over your toes and place your weight in your heels for 1 minute. Then, do alternating side lunges – 20 reps on each side. Finally, here is an example of how to do a burpee where you will do 15 reps. Jump down into a pushup and then jump in the air as fast as you can. Repeat this 4 times for a great cardio workout!
