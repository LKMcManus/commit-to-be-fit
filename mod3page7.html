Articles Main Take-a-ways

  • Proteins contain essential and non-essential Amino Acids that are body uses up during exercise.
  • Foods that are high in protein are: fish, poultry, eggs, legumes, nuts and milk products.
  • Similar to taking in less calories than you burn to lose fat weight. You have to take in a higher level of protein that you burn to gain muscle.
  • There are benefits to consuming protein before and after strength training.
  • You should consume 0.35 grams of protein per pound of body weight per day for general health.
